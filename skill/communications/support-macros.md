# Comms: Support Macros

Ready, safe replies for the questions support and moderators get during an
incident. Each keeps users safe, avoids overclaiming, and never asks for
secrets. Adapt the bracketed parts; keep the safety lines intact.

## "I had a failed transaction"

```text
Sorry for the trouble. A failed transaction usually means it didn't complete on
-chain, so funds typically aren't lost. Can you share the transaction signature
and the time? Please don't share your seed phrase or private key with anyone.
```

## "My funds are missing"

```text
Thank you for flagging this — we're taking it seriously. Please share the
transaction signature, the wallet app, and the website URL you used. Do not
share your seed phrase or private key. We'll never DM you first or ask for them.
```

## "I connected my wallet"

```text
Connecting a wallet alone doesn't move funds — signing a transaction does. Did
you approve or sign anything? If a prompt looked wrong, disconnect and stop
signing. Which exact URL did you use?
```

## "Is this a hack?"

```text
We're investigating and don't want to guess before we have evidence. Here's what
we know so far: [accurate, scoped statement]. We'll post updates at [channel].
Please rely only on official channels and ignore DMs claiming to be support.
```

## "Someone DM'd me support"

```text
That is not us. We never DM users first and never ask for your seed phrase or
private keys. Please don't click their links or share anything. Block and report
them, and use only our official site and channels.
```

## "Should I revoke approvals?"

```text
Reviewing and revoking token approvals you don't recognize is generally good
hygiene, using a tool you trust and reaching it yourself (not via a DM link).
Never enter your seed phrase to "revoke" anything — that's a scam pattern.
```

## "When will this be fixed?"

```text
We don't have a confirmed time yet and don't want to promise one we can't keep.
We're posting updates at [channel] and our next update is by [time]. Thank you
for your patience.
```

## Rules

No promises of reimbursement, no blame, no "resolved"/"funds are safe" without
evidence, and never a request for secrets. See
[`public-status-update.md`](public-status-update.md) and
[`../modes/user-safety-mode.md`](../modes/user-safety-mode.md).
